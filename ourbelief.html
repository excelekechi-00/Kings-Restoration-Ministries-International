✦ The Core of Our Faith

What We Believe

At Kings Restoration Ministries International, our faith is anchored in the eternal truth of God's Word. These are the foundational beliefs that guide our worship, teaching, and community life.

01

The Holy Scripture

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God. It is the supreme standard for all matters of faith, doctrine, and Christian living.

2 Timothy 3:16–17

02

The Holy Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three Persons — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — equal in nature, distinct in Person, and unified in purpose.

Matthew 28:19 · 2 Corinthians 13:14

03

Salvation by Grace

We believe salvation is a gift of God's grace, received through faith in Jesus Christ alone. It cannot be earned by human effort, but only through repentance and trust in Christ.

Ephesians 2:8–9 · Romans 10:9

04

Divine Healing & Restoration

We believe God heals today — spirit, soul, and body. Restoration is central to our identity, grounded in His promise to restore health and bring wholeness to His people.

Jeremiah 30:17 · Isaiah 53:5

"I will restore health unto you, said the LORD God."
